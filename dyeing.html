Dyeing

We offer two kinds of dyeing : Natural dyeing and Low-impact dyeing

Natural dyeing

Dyeing with the use of only plants and minerals is something we have studied and practiced on a large scale. A majority of the natural dye is sourced from plants- root, berries, bark, leaves and wood.

Low-impact dyeing

Use of Azo free dyes is another sustainable dyeing option available. Low-impact or Eco friendly dyes do not contain toxic metals, chemicals or mordants, needs less rinsing and has high absorption rate which finally results in less water waste.

Color Shades

Natural dyeing

A full palette of colours can be achieved using natural dyes and extracts, although it is very difficult to achieve the exact same results every time with natural dyes. We make use of a wide range of 30 different natural dye powders and extracts to dye the fabrics or yarns to beautiful natural colors. For Shades of Yellow and Orange: Himalayan Rhubarb, Henna, Marigold For Shades of Red: Madder, Kamela, Lac For Shades of Blue: Indigo For Shades of Brown: Cutch To get the right color we make use of combinations of different natural dyes.

Low-impact dyeing

Any color can be achieved using azo-free dyes. We use pantone colors in TPG and TPX format to match the fabrics to the exact colors.
