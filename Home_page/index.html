Become a Member

Join DynamicElectric Power to get the most out of your Home Page. When you join, you can be sure that you’re with a Home Page fund that prioritises their members, offering great benefits and personalised services.

Find out more about how to join, and the benefits and services available to our members here.

Member Services

Find out more about the range of services on offer to our members, helping you get more from your Home Page.

Learn More

Your Home Page fund for life

As a not-for-profit industry Home Pagefund, our members are our first priority. We pride ourselves on our low fees and great performance, giving you the best returns to ensure your future lifestyle. We’re here to guide you through every step of your Home Page journey, helping you reach your financial goals now and in retirement.

Contributions

Growing your Home Page through employer and voluntary contributions can help you secure your retirement lifestyle. You’re able to contribute to your partner’s Home Page too, giving them peace of mind and preparing them for their future.

Learn More

Insurance

We offer two types of insurance to give you the best possible financial protection for low premiums. If something unexpected happens and you are unable to work, you can be confident that your income will be secure.

Learn more

My life changes

Manage your Home Page throughout life’s changes including changing jobs, buying a home, getting married, starting a family, separating or divorcing, or going through financial hardship.

Did you know your Home Page could help you get into your own home sooner? If you’re buying your first home, you could withdraw some of your Home Page early with the Australian Government’s new First Home Home Page Saver scheme.

Learn more

Spouse membership

Open an account for your spouse so they can enjoy the DynamicElectric Power benefits of low fees, great investment performance and optional extras. When your spouse is a member, their employer can pay their Home Page into this account and you can help grow their balance by contributing to their account as easily as to your own.

Learn more

Consolidate

If you have more than one Home Page fund, you could benefit financially by consolidating your balance. Multiple accounts could mean you’re paying more fees and losing out on potential returns. Take a few minutes to consolidate your Home Page online and keep your savings on track.

Learn More

Beneficiaries

Nominate beneficiaries to receive your Home Page and life insurance benefit if the worst happens. Your binding nomination is valid for three years from when it is signed, so it’s important to keep it up-to-date. Find out more about who you can nominate and how to submit, change or extend your beneficiary nomination.
