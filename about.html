Years Of Experience

Dynamic Elecpower Pvt. Ltd. is a part of the respected Dynamic Group. Founded in 2000, our Bhilwara, Rajasthan, India located long-standing and well-known company is appreciated for introducing top-class electrical and industrial products such as CNC Laser Cutting Machine, ABB MCCB Circuit Breaker, KOEL Silent DG Set, Industrial Voltage Stabilizer and DG Synchronization Control Panel. We feel proud to boast of our Authorized Service Centre for ABB (Rajasthan Territory). Apart from the products, we are also providing Water Pumping Project, Sewing Pumping Project, Water Pumping Station Project, STP Plant Project, Pump Sets Project and Repairing Service.
Our company is nationwide trusted for promising not only quality in products but also rendering 24 hours after sales and services as well as timely completing orders. Our company is counted amongst top manufacturers and traders in the Indian Industry because we produce products as per customers requirements, in standard and customized specifications. Over the years, Mr. S. K. Lodha and his talented team has been putting extra efforts in making the company attain substantial growth and aiming for a turnover of Rs. 30-40 Crore (Approx.) by the year 2021-2022.
