OUR SERVICES

We stock a wide range of fabrics, including natural woven and knits fabrics, with an emphasis on fabrics that are environmentally and socially responsible. Ultimately, we hope to offer creators the resources and materials to bring to imagination to life.

Fabric Sourcing

We provide a wide variety of natural and sustainable fabrics like hemp, linen, organic cotton, wild-silk, modal, bamboo, etc. You can have these fabrics in natural/greige, ready-for-dye or in dyed form. Depending on your quantity, we can also produce many different types of woven and knit fabrics according to your requirement.

See our fabrics

Dyeing And Printing Services

We provide custom dyeing services for any of our fabrics with no MOQ for azo-free dyeing and MOQ as low as 50 metres for Natural Dyeing. We also offer screen printing, digital printing, block printing, tie-dye services. Apart from dyeing and printing, we offer fabric processing services like bio-enzyme washing, laser cutting, embroidery, smocking, heat setting, etc.

Product Development And Tailoring

We can help bring your product ideas to life, from Children’s and Women’s clothing to accessories and home furnishings. We have a dedicated team for product development to help you through the process till final production. Once the pattern and the prototype is in hand we start sampling for the required product. We can develop any style based on the patterns and tech-pack. We can stitch both woven and knitted fabrics.

Extra Services

We provide other services too besides manufacturing, like branding, labeling, quality checking, packaging, exporting, developing tools for printing, special fabric weaving, specific yarn development, etc.
